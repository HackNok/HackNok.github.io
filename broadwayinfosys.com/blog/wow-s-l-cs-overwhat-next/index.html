WOW S.L.C’s over……What next?

Study plan, charts, revision, multiple exams, tuitions, parents’ concern, prayers, a lot of preparation, confidence, tension, pressure and then………. S.L.C.  Now, it’s finally over. What a relief!!! , at least until the results are out, isn’t it? What next then?  High school; simple answer. But, here we are asking what to do next in the gap before the results are out or you join college?

You guys are smart enough to think and plan about what to do next in the gap. Obviously, fun element has a huge piece in your plan pie-chart. And we don’t deny with it. You guys need to have fun ….after all you have longed for it.  At the meantime, your guardians, relatives, teachers, well-wishers might have already started giving you suggestions regarding what to do and make this time more productive. Most of them may be busy asking you and in fact telling you what to study and become in future, about scope, opportunities…etc., isn’t it? Some of you might consider this trend old-fashioned, and think why these people don’t want us to have fun and chill out? Well, the answer is they all have gone through this and we consider them not old-fashioned but experienced enough to let you know about the situations they have to face in their life; both positive and negative. Some of them may have success stories to be proud about while some regrets for not thinking productively when they had to.

So guys, relax and have fun. However, at the same time give few hours of your day to exercise your brain with something interesting to learn and use in your days ahead. Remember, nothing is permanent in this world except change. Everything changes, even job prospects, education and tough competition is everywhere. You need to prove yourself in every step ahead and for that you need to first take a good DECISION…….. a decision to develop yourself.

Whatever decision you take regarding your career in future, you need to think and find out what is common and very essential in whatever you do? IT…Yes, Information Technology was, is and will always be one of the most important and now inseparable aspects of our life and career. Now, you may think you all are experts in computer… more than a generation before. Right? We agree with this fact. But, here we are not talking about, computer basics, power point, office application or use of internet. We have more for you. Yes, there is not a hard and fast rule that you can study IT only after you are in college or when you have chosen IT as your subject to study. You can learn and exercise it whenever YOU and YOUR MIND is READY.

Now, after you decide to take some of your valuable fun time to utilize in studying IT, the next question that will haunt your mind is: What to study in IT and where? You have lots of choices in the market. There are institutes waiting to admit you with lots of offers. It again depends upon you which institute to choose and where will you get a proper guidance along with opportunities to shape your career and learning path.

Let us (Broadway Infosys Nepal) help you. We won’t start mentioning here who we are, from when are we involved in this sector and what are our vision, mission or plans. You can go through the website for it if you want to know. Visit us and we will mention only what you want to know and what will help you decide to join our hands and take a step ahead. How can you start? What will be beneficial to you? What projects will you do? How will the learning experience be advantageous to you? What opportunities we provide to you after and during the course? There are lots to know and lots to share. So, what are you waiting for?
