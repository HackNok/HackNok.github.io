Importance of Cisco Certification (CCNA) Training in Nepal

July 29, 2017 No Comments

Cisco Certified Network Associate is the full form of CCNA. CCNA is the composite exam associated with the Cisco Certified Network Associate Routing & Switching certification. This exam aims to test a candidate’s knowledge and skills required to install, operate, and troubleshoot a small to medium size enterprise branch network. Hence, the importance of CCNA is very high.

The importance of CCNA is high in the field of Information technology because professionals with CCNA are taken as more trustworthy. It shows that the person is worthy to be handled with responsibility. CCNA adds a serious incentive to your resume so the importance of CCNA has grown even more. Additionally, Cisco is regarded as the most trustworthy alternatives when it comes to IT-Networking so people prefer CCNA over others Also, the majority of the organizations have Cisco equipment. This is yet another reason that raised the importance of CCNA.

CCNA explains the basics of networking as it explains the fundamental concepts more clearly. CCNA plays the major role of being a prerequisite of other popular courses like CCNP. CCNA is advisable to all of them who have an interest in the field of networking and Information Technology. Earlier the better. Fortunately, if you get CCNA earlier in the career, you will have a wide range of options to take upon. Some of them include Security, Data Center, Service Provider, wireless etc. Actually, the early CCNA gives you more time to work in your field of interest with professionalism.

The importance of CCNA does not end here. CCNA is necessary when you want to grow in your already working field in Information Technology, in your own company or by switching to other company. CCNA certifies you and it actually puts a tag on you that prove that you have knowledge about the subject. Companies will have a legitimate proof that could be considered in the case of promoting you or hiring you in case you are watching the company. In either of the cases, CCNA proves your intelligence and knowledge.

However, CCNA is not always the hero. Of course, it is preferable and is important for your knowledge, promotion and the like but there are certain situations where others are more preferable than CCNA. For example, if you are already working or having the interest to work on server implying the storage side of the network, then other certifications are recommended rather than CCNA. However, the decision depends upon OS you are working on.
